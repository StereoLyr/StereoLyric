Losses are sad, losing a mother, a friend, even your own life seems to abandon you, leaving you on the verge of thinking about leaving this world. After trying to escape to a quiet refuge in her new high school, Hayashi Ren finds a tall, young, elite pianist who only seeks to hide from the spotlight, but continues to play with his heart.

At first, they only shared the abandoned classroom to sleep or hide from people, but little by little she meets people who seem to have a good, admirable, and healthy life... or so she thinks. When she returns from high school, she sees on the news an announcement about the new idol group M✕✕N becoming increasingly famous and expected to perform on the biggest stage in the country by the end of the year.

As Ren was about to walk past, the news played a clip of the group's song. Upon recognizing the lyrics and the leader's voice, there was no doubt that it was that friend he had lost a few years ago. He had a peculiar idea: to reach the same stage as them to reunite with her, Miyu.

To that end, he decides to form a band because being a solo bassist wasn't going to be very helpful, and he'll try to create a cohesive group, or at least not die before they manage to write their first song. Literally.
